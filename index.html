Frequently Asked Questions

When does the app launch?

Open beta starts early 2026 for waitlist members. Full launch expected Spring 2026.

Is Endur Fitness free?

Endur Fitness is free to use! All runners get core tracking and competitions at no cost. Upgrade to premium for extra features.

Which devices and apps sync?

Works with Apple and Android devices, plus manual entry for full flexibility.

Can I track activities on my own?

Yes! You can use the Track option in the app to log activities without competing.

Are there rewards?

Yes! Recognition, premium challenges, future merch, and in-app rewards!
